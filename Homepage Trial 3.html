个人简介

Biography

杨洋，北京师范大学教育科技中心副教授。研究方向包括科学/STEM教育、 教育测量与评价。当前重点关注生成式人工智能辅助的科学教学与学习、 在线科学探究平台设计、大规模科学测评以及非正式科学学习。

Dr. Yang Yang is an Associate Professor at Beijing Normal University. His research areas include Science/STEM Education and Educational Measurement. His current research focuses on GenAI-assisted science teaching and learning, online science inquiry platforms, large-scale science assessment, and informal science learning.
