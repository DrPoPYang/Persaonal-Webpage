Dr. Yang Yang is an Associate Professor at the Center for Educational Science and Technology, Beijing Normal University. His research areas include Science/STEM education and Educational Measurement and Evaluation. His current research focuses on GenAI-assisted Science Teaching and Learning, the Design and Implementation of Online Science Inquiry Platforms, Large-scale Science Assessment, and Informal Science Learning. In addition, he has five years of frontline teaching and management experience in A-level/GCSE Physics and Statistics. Since 2013, he has participated in over 20 research projects in the capacity of data analysis expert, curriculum design specialist, or project evaluation expert. In terms of teaching, he has designed and taught multiple undergraduate and graduate courses, such as Academic Reading and Writing, Educational Measurement and Evaluation, Research Methods in the Social Sciences, and Informal Science Education.
